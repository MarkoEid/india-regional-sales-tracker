## 📂 Dataset Information
* **Source:** [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/) (Nestle India Sales Dataset)
* **Domain:** Fast-Moving Consumer Goods (FMCG) / Confectionery
* **Geography:** India (Regional & State-level data)

### 📋 Column Glossary & Data Dictionary
| Column Name | Description |
| :--- | :--- |
| **State** | The specific Indian state where the transaction occurred (e.g., Maharashtra, Delhi). |
| **Region** | Geographic grouping of states (North, West, East, South). |
| **Product / SKU** | The name of the specific Nestle product sold (e.g., Smarties, Milky Bar). |
| **Price** | The monetary value of the transaction (Total Revenue). |
| **Units** | The quantity of items sold in a single transaction. |
| **Date** | The timestamp of the sale, used for identifying monthly and seasonal trends. |
| **%GT Sum of Price** | A calculated field showing the percentage contribution of a product to total revenue. |

## 📊 Executive Summary
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of **3M in total revenue** and **34K units sold**. It highlights key performance indicators (KPIs) to help stakeholders understand which regions and products are driving growth.

| :--- | :--- |

## 🔍 Visual Breakdowns

### 1. Geographic Performance
* **Top States:** Maharashtra (542K) and Delhi (492K) are the primary revenue drivers.
* **Regional Split:** * **North:** 35.1% (Market Leader)
    * **West:** 31.2%
    * **East:** 17.1%
    * **South:** 16.6%

### 2. Product Contribution
The product portfolio shows a healthy distribution, with the top 3 SKUs accounting for over 41% of total sales:
* **Nestle Smarties Pop-Up:** 15.8%
* **Nestle Milky Bar Stick:** 13.1%
* **Nestle Fab:** 12.7%
  
### 3. Sales Seasonality
The **Monthly Trend** area chart reveals a steady baseline through Q1-Q3, followed by a **massive exponential surge in November and December**. This suggests a strong correlation with the Indian festive season and year-end gifting.

## 📈 Strategic Insights
1. **Seasonal Readiness:** The supply chain must be optimized for a 10x volume increase in November/December based on the historical trend.
2. **Expansion Opportunity:** There is significant room for growth in the **South** and **East** regions, which currently trail behind the North and West.
3. **Product Focus:** "Nestle Smarties Pop-Up" is the flagship product; maintaining its shelf availability is critical for hitting revenue targets.
