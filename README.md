## 🔍 Visual Breakdowns

### 1. Geographic Performance
* **Top States:** Maharashtra (542K) and Delhi (492K) are the primary revenue drivers.
* **Regional Split:** * **North:** 35.1% (Market Leader)
    * **West:** 31.2%
    * **East:** 17.1%
    * **South:** 16.6%

### 2. Product Contribution
The product portfolio shows a healthy distribution, with the top 3 SKUs accounting for over 41% of total sales:
* **Nestle Smarties Pop-Up:** 15.8%
* **Nestle Milky Bar Stick:** 13.1%
* **Nestle Fab:** 12.7%
  
### 3. Sales Seasonality
The **Monthly Trend** area chart reveals a steady baseline through Q1-Q3, followed by a **massive exponential surge in November and December**. This suggests a strong correlation with the Indian festive season and year-end gifting.

## 📈 Strategic Insights
1. **Seasonal Readiness:** The supply chain must be optimized for a 10x volume increase in November/December based on the historical trend.
2. **Expansion Opportunity:** There is significant room for growth in the **South** and **East** regions, which currently trail behind the North and West.
3. **Product Focus:** "Nestle Smarties Pop-Up" is the flagship product; maintaining its shelf availability is critical for hitting revenue targets.
